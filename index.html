About

Analytics that lives
outside the
dashboard.

I'm an analytics consultant with five years of experience helping financial services companies make faster, better-grounded decisions. My work spans business analytics, customer segmentation, propensity modelling, and scaled data products — built across Mastercard Advisors, American Express, and Bain Capability Network.

What I care about most is the distance between an insight and a decision. In large organisations, that distance is often enormous — models sit in notebooks, dashboards go unread, and strategy teams operate on gut feel. I've spent my career shortening that gap: owning work end-to-end, from raw data to the recommendation that lands in a business meeting.

I'm now applying that domain depth to AI — building LLM-powered tools for personal finance, and targeting Series A–D fintech and AI startups where analytics directly shapes product and strategy decisions, not just reports.

Based in Gurgaon. Open to remote-first roles with global teams.
